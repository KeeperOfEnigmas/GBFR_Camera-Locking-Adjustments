v 0.1.0:
> Adjust hard lock target selection.
    - Now prioritize targets that are close to the center of the screen rather than close to player.
    - Very far away targets are still excluded. 
    - If multiple targets stack together in the center of the screen, the one that is relatively close to you will be locked.
    - Doesn't affect selection of target parts.
> 调整了硬锁的目标选择逻辑。
    - 现在优先锁定屏幕中间的目标，而不是距离比较近的。
    - 非常远的目标不会被锁。
    - 如果多个目标在中间叠了起来，会选择一个相对比较近的。
    - 不影响目标部位的选择。
